  • About us

    Dextra is the Annual Technical Festival of Jaypee University Of Engineering And Technology.
    A collaboration of, for and by tech enthusiasts, Dextra is a platform for technocrats to design, code and build innovative solutions with an envision to transform India into a digitally empowered society and a knowledge based economy, all the while providing a venue for self-expression and creativity. It boosts a plethora of intensive, gripping technical events organised by ISF, community of electronics hobbyists and enthusiasts within JUET.
    ISF is home to innovative minds. It conducts various events and sessions throughout the year to bring out the techie in you. Our vision is to nurture your engineering maturity by giving you practical and hands-on exposure. We aim to fill in the void of Electronics hobbyists in the institute by providing a platform where the theory learnt in classes can be applied.
    Come, let's join a new world and explore a new you.
